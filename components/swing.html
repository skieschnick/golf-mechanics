Swing

Let's understand the swing mechanics

Getting your sequencing is key to power and limited impact on the body

  1. The takeaway, key not "breaking" left arm
  2. Backswing club plane with weight shifting to inside right foot, if at all
  3. Transition witha slight pause, hands move down BEFORE shoulders start rotating
  4. Downswing is key with holding the angle
  5. Impact/Release is AFTER the ball
  6. Follow-through at the target well balanced and limited, if any, lateral movement
